Privacy Policy - Driver App

Effective Date: 08/13/2025

This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data as a registered driver in the Team Car app. By using the app, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Data We Collect
We collect the following data during registration or while using the app:

Full name

Phone number

Residential address

Driver's license and a copy of it

Vehicle data (license plate number, vehicle type, year of manufacture, vehicle category)

Geolocation data while on duty

Any legally required supporting documents

2. How We Use the Data
Your data is used for the following purposes:

Verifying your eligibility to provide transportation services

Managing your account and ensuring its security

Matching you with suitable passengers

Communicating with you about updates or service-related issues

Complying with legal and regulatory obligations

3. Data Sharing

Your geolocation data may be shared with passengers during a trip

We do not sell or rent your data to third parties

4. Data Protection

We use technical and administrative protection systems to prevent unauthorized access

You are responsible for keeping your password confidential and not sharing it

5. Data Retention Period

We retain your data as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide the service

You may contact us via email: teamcar.jo@gmail.com or through the app
